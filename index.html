Our Story

A Legacy of Healing in Hubballi

Kshema Physiotherapy & Chiropractic Centre was established with a singular vision: to bring world-class, non-invasive pain management to North Karnataka. Founded by Dr. Vasudev Vader (MPT), the clinic has evolved from a modest consulting room to a state-of-the-art rehabilitation facility.

Our Philosophy: We believe that the body has an innate ability to heal when aligned correctly. Unlike traditional approaches that rely heavily on medication, our "Hybrid Therapy" model integrates the structural precision of Chiropractic care with the muscular rehabilitation of Physiotherapy.
